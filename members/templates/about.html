Welcome To HBK & Associates

HBK & Associates LLP (HBK) is a professional firm offering a complete package of top notch services covering all aspects of Audit & Assurance, Taxation, Corporate Law and various Entrepreneurial Services for all sectors of businesses whether it may be large or small.

HBK started its journey in 2011 and since then, its being functional in various sectors providing quality services to its clients in a professional & timely manner. Our team (team strength 90+) consists of 3 Partners, 8 Chartered Accountants, 3 Company Secretary, and manyGraduates, Post graduates and ArticleAssistants.

We support our clients, whether it may be a startups, Alternative Investment Funds, NRI Clients, Off-shore Institutions, private equity, etc. in terms of incorporations, registrations, accounting, tax & regulatory compliances, all under one roof.

We have well-competent team with vast knowledge, excellent expertise and practical industry experience in broad range of sectors including financial services, banking, logistics, education, travel, media, etc.
